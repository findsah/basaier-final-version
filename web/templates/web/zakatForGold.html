The Zakat (Islamic Charity) is among the most important acts of worship in Islam, it is indeed its third pillar. Allah the Almighty made it mandatory to give Zakat in a set time and method. We shall not postpone the payment nor add heresies in it. Unfortunately, several Muslims fail to allot this mandatory charity its due importance; they usually procrastinate in performing it or ignore the right way to do so. In this article, we will clarify the method to calculate the Zakat of all sorts of wealth.
